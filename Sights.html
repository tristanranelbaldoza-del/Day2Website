One drawing,
one-and-a-half centuries.

Antoni Gaudí took over the project in 1883 and worked on it for forty-three years — the last fifteen of them exclusively — until his death in 1926. We're still finishing what he left behind.

01 · Started 1882

First stone laid on the 19th of March, under architect Francisco de Paula del Villar.

02 · Height 172.5m

Planned height of the central Jesus tower — one metre shy of Montjuïc, so no human work exceeds God's.

03 · Spires 18

Total planned spires. Twelve apostles, four evangelists, one Mary, one Christ — eight built as of 2026.

04 · Finish 2026

Target consecration year — a century after Gaudí was struck by a tram and laid to rest inside the crypt.
